Our Story

A Family Tradition

From Naples to Mumbai, our journey spans generations of culinary excellence and unwavering dedication to authentic Italian flavors

38+
Years of Excellence

Our story begins in the sun-drenched hills of Naples, where our great-grandparents cultivated the recipes that would become our legacy. Each dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the perfect harmony of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Today in Mumbai, we continue this proud tradition, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary culinary artistry to create unforgettable dining experiences.

1920s — The Roots

Our ancestors crafted simple yet delicious dishes in Naples using only the freshest local produce.

1985 — Mumbai Arrival

Bringing the taste of Naples to Mumbai, we opened our first family-owned restaurant, delighting locals with authentic flavors.

Today — Culinary Excellence

With award-winning chefs and a passion for innovation, we continue to craft memorable experiences while honoring tradition.
